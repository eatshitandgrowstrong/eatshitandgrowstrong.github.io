Bill of materials

Things I might need:

  1. Solder
    • For soldering components to stripboard
  2. Copper Stripboard
  3. 5V water pump
    • For dispensing water to the plants
  4. Transistors
    • To be used as a switch, allows the UNO output to be used as a signal, instead of directly driving the waterpump
    • Maybe rated for 0.2A? Depends on water pump supplied
  5. Diodes
    • For freewheeling diode in water pump circuit, to prevent reverse EMF from damaging the water pump
  6. Acrylic for engraving
  7. Push Buttons
    • Inputs for activating the system, manual watering etc
  8. LEDs
    • Individual Colored LEDS for what state the system is in, for instance on or off.
    • RGB LED
  9. Resistors
    • 1kΩ, for LEDs
  10. DHT 22
    • DHT22 accurate to 0.5°C whereas DHT11 is accurate to 2°C, huge difference
  11. Buzzer or Speaker
      Not too sure on speaker programming but am pretty competent with buzzer tunes, use buzzer to play a tune or annoying pitch when water tank is low on water.
  12. Wires
    • Could use some wires for jumper cables or dupont cables if exclusively working on breadboard
  13. Breadboards
    • Maybe additional breadboards if not soldering? Breadboard exclusive project
  14. 9V adapter
  15. Dupont Cables
    • Female to Male Cables, for interfacing boards with Arduino Ports
  16. Breadboard headers
    • For female to male dupont cables interfacing with Arduino ports
