Computer Aided Design (CAD) Part 3

In this part of CAD I'll be documenting how I made my keychain with CAD.

I first start by sketching a base.

I then extruded the height of the keychain.

I then sketched the keychain hole and areas I would cutting/removing.

I then used the previous sketches to extrude negatively, cutting/removing material, making way for the hole for the keychain.

I then removed some of the material around the keychain hole.

I then used the fillet tool to make smoothen the corners, making it one continous surface instead of 2 pointy corners.

I then did the same for the 4 corners of the keychain body.

After this, I used the fillet tool on the top surface.

Afterwards, I sketched my name on the top of the keychain surface.

Finally, I extruded my name, resulting in my name being visible.
