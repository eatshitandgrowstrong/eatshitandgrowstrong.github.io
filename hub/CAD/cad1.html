What is CAD?

CAD stands for Computer Aided Design, like the name suggests, CAD is the use of computers to help with the designing process, increasing the productivity of its user.

Modelling an egg cup is a good illustration of the usefulness of CAD software.

For instance, if I want to model an egg cup, there are two tools, revolve and shell that would greatly reduce my workload.

First, I sketch half of the cross section of the egg cup. I sketch half the cross section since I would like it to revolve along the

I use the revolve command here to do a 360 degree revolve along the Z axis, creating a solid object.

I then proceed to use the shell tool on top surface of the solid egg cup, this results in a shell with the thickness here being set to 5mm.

And just like that, I have a modelled egg cup in no time.
